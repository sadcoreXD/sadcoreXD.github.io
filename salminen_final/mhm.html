About Vr Surgery


Tradition since like the 80s

Virtual Reality (VR) is a new cutting edge technology that submerges you into a computer generated world. It is used in many different ways, but today we are going to focus on its uses in the healthcare industry.

VR has many medical uses, one of which is to help surgeons prepare for surgeries. VR allows surgeons to virtually perform surgeries to improve their abilities. Doctors use Vr to also experience what it is like for their patients so they can properly empathise. Some doctors use this to know what their dementia patients experience
